## Project Purpose
We are building the public-facing website for Hudson Helm, a small MSP serving Northern New Jersey / NYC-area small businesses (typically 5–25 users, but credible up to ~50).

The site must feel modern, polished, and trustworthy—without pretending to be a large enterprise.
